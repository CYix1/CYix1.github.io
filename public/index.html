Definition

note: mostly [pcgbook]

Procedual Content Generation (PCG): PCG is the algorithmic creation of game content with limited or indirect user input. In other words, PCG refers to computer software that can create game content on its own, or together with one or many human players or designers. [pcgbook]

Content: includes …

  • levels
  • maps
  • game rules
  • textures
  • stories
  • items
  • quests
  • music
  • weapons
  • vehicles
  • characters
  • etc.

NPC AI does not count towards content according to [pcgbook]
